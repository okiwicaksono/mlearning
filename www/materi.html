Fact VS Opinion

Fact is something that has really occurred or is actually the case. Whereas an opinion are mostly subjective which means that they are not based on absolute fact but on emotions and interpretation of facts.

For example, look at the picture!

Fact : Floods hit the town. (reality, based on photo)

Opinion : Many people don’t care about their town. (not real, only people’s opinion)

Quiz 1:

Where are the facts, and where are the opinions?
