Petunjuk Belajar / Directions

  • Baca dan pahami Tujuan Belajar sebelum membuka menu Materi
  • Pelajarilah materi secara urut
  • Pahami setiap materi dengan baik, ulangi hingga kamu benar-benar menguasainya
  • Pastikan kamu sudah memahami semua materi, selanjutnya kamu bisa masuk ke menu evaluasi untuk mengukur tingkat pemahaman materi ini.
  • Jangan bosan untuk belajar, karena kesuksesan bukanlah hal instan melainkan rutinitas.

Tujuan Belajar / Objectives

By exploring this unit, students are expected to :
  • Antusiasm to learn English and be grateful to God about English as an international language.
  • Demonstrate honest, disciplinary, responsible, and confident conducts to carry out transactional communication with teachers and peers.
  • Analyze the social function, generic structure, and language elements of giving opinion.
  • Arrange simple oral and written texts to give opinion.
  • Inquire various ways of giving opinion as well as their differences from those in Indonesian.
  • Demonstrate and be engaged in various activities of inquiring and giving opinion.
  • Have knowledge about the use of tag questions.
  • Understand main ideas, specific information, and detailed information in hortatory exposition texts.
