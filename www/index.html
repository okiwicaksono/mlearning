Goals:

By exploring these matters, you are expected to:
  • Enthusiasm to learn English and be grateful to God about English as an international language.
  • Self-confidence regarding communicating with teachers and peers.
  • Analyze the social function and language elements of giving opinion.
  • Arrange simple oral and written texts to give opinion.
  • Inquire various ways of giving opinion
  • Demonstrate and be engaged in various activities of inquiring and giving opinion.
  • Have knowledge about the use of tag questions.
  • Understand main ideas and specific information in texts.
